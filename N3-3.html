N3-3 Vocabulary Quiz (1-25)

Topic: Commuting / Going to work (Slide 1/5)
Q1. I have to go to work by 9:00 AM every day. The word for "going to work" is...
Hint: "Shu" means "going out" or "attending", and "kin" means "work".
Answer: A. 出勤 (shukkin)
Q2. The train is always full of people commuting in the morning.
Hint: It means "full capacity" or "crowded with people".
Answer: A. 満員 (man'in)
Q3. Because of an accident, the train is ______.
Hint: Used for transportation delays.
Answer: B. 遅延 (chien)
Q4. I will ______ from my house at 8:00 AM to go to the office.
Hint: Means "to depart" or "to leave".
Answer: A. 出発 (shuppatsu)
Q5. I forgot my commuter pass, so I had to buy a ______.
Hint: A ticket used for trains or buses.
Answer: B. 切符 (kippu)
Topic: Actions at the workplace (Slide 2/5)
Q6. I need to make a copy of these ______.
Hint: Documents or materials used for work.
Answer: B. 資料 (shiryou)
Q7. My computer is broken, so I have to ______ it.
Hint: To repair or fix something.
Answer: A. 修理する (shuuri suru)
Q8. Please ______ your desk before leaving.
Hint: To tidy up or put away.
Answer: B. 片付ける (katadzukeru)
Q9. I asked my boss for ______ to take a day off.
Hint: Permission or approval.
Answer: A. 許可 (kyoka)
Q10. We have to finish this task by the ______.
Hint: The deadline or due date.
Answer: A. 締め切り (shimekiri)
Topic: Meetings / Discussions (Slide 3/5)
Q11. The meeting is scheduled to ______ at 2 PM.
Hint: To start or begin.
Answer: B. 始まる (hajimaru)
Q12. I need to ______ a room for the meeting.
Hint: To reserve or book.
Answer: A. 予約する (yoyaku suru)
Q13. In the meeting, we discussed the new project's ______.
Hint: Plan or schedule.
Answer: A. 計画 (keikaku)
Q14. Can I ask a ______ about this slide?
Hint: A question.
Answer: B. 質問 (shitsumon)
Q15. Everyone agreed with my ______.
Hint: A proposal or suggestion.
Answer: B. 提案 (teian)
Topic: Clients / Business trips (Slide 4/5)
Q16. Tomorrow, I will go on a ______ to Osaka.
Hint: A business trip.
Answer: A. 出張 (shucchou)
Q17. When meeting a new client, you should exchange ______.
Hint: Business cards.
Answer: B. 名刺 (meishi)
Q18. I have an ______ with the manager at 3 PM.
Hint: An appointment or promise.
Answer: A. 約束 (yakusoku)
Q19. We signed a new ______ with the company.
Hint: A contract or agreement.
Answer: A. 契約 (keiyaku)
Q20. He works in the ______ department and visits many clients.
Hint: Sales or business operations.
Answer: A. 営業 (eigyou)
Topic: Leaving work / Overtime (Slide 5/5)
Q21. Because there is a lot of work, I have to do ______ tonight.
Hint: Overtime work.
Answer: A. 残業 (zangyou)
Q22. My boss told me, "Good work today", which in Japanese is "______".
Hint: A phrase used to thank someone for their hard work.
Answer: B. お疲れ様でした (otsukaresama deshita)
Q23. It's time to ______ work and go home.
Hint: To finish or end.
Answer: B. 終わる (owaru)
Q24. Before leaving, I must send a ______ to my team.
Hint: A report.
Answer: B. 報告 (houkoku)
Q25. I am tired, so I want to go to bed ______ tonight.
Hint: Early or quickly.
Answer: A. 早く (hayaku)
