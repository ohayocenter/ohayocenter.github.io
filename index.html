My Journey

A lifelong lover of travel, I spent my 20s and 30s exploring diverse cultures across Asia, North America, and Europe. The kindness I received from people worldwide inspired my deep fascination with the power of communication.

Now in my 40s, I balance my professional life as a stay-at-home father with my passion for teaching one-on-one Japanese lessons online at Ohayo Center. This role keeps me grounded, patient, and focused on helping others grow.
