猫の手も借りたい

ねこのてもかりたい

Want to borrow even a cat's paw

"Extremely busy"

So busy you'd take help from anyone.
年末は忙しくて、猫の手も借りたい状況だ。 (I'm so busy at the end of the year, I'd take any help I can get.)

サバを読む

さばをよむ

Reading the mackerel

"To fudge numbers"

Manipulating counts or age to one's advantage.
彼は年齢を２歳ほどサバを読んでいる。 (He is fudging his age by about two years.)

頭が固い

あたまがかたい

Hard head

"Stubborn / Inflexible"

Unwilling to change ideas or ways.
彼は頭が固くて、新しい方法を認めようとしない。 (He is so stubborn that he won't accept new methods.)

やきもちを焼く

やきもちをやく

Grilling mochi

"To be jealous"

Feeling romantic jealousy.
彼女が他の人と楽しそうに話していると、彼はやきもちを焼く。 (He gets jealous when she talks happily with others.)

耳が痛い

みみがいたい

Ears are aching

"Hard to hear the truth"

Finding a direct criticism painful to hear.
生活習慣を注意されて、耳が痛い。 (It's hard to hear the truth when someone warns me about my lifestyle.)

水に流す

みずにながす

Let it flow into the water

"Forgive and forget"

To wash away past grudges.
喧嘩のことは水に流して、仲直りしよう。 (Let's forgive and forget the fight and make up.)

朝飯前

あさめしまえ

Before breakfast

"Piece of cake"

Something very easy to finish.
この程度の修理なら朝飯前だよ。 (A simple repair like this is a piece of cake.)

骨を折る

ほねをおる

Breaking a bone

"Take great pains"

To work very hard on something.
交渉のためにずいぶんと骨を折った。 (I took great pains to handle the negotiations.)

油を売る

あぶらをうる

Selling oil

"To idle away time"

Spending time chatting instead of working.
仕事の途中で油を売っていて、怒られた。 (I got scolded for idling away my time during work.)

根回し

ねまわし

Digging around the roots

"Laying groundwork"

Building informal consensus beforehand.
会議の前に根回しをしておいた。 (I laid the groundwork before the meeting.)

顔が広い

かおがひろい

Wide face

"Well-connected"

Knowing many influential people.
彼は政界に顔が広い。 (He is well-connected in the political world.)

猫をかぶる

ねこをかぶる

Wearing a cat

"Playing innocent"

Feigning gentleness or ignorance.
彼は家では荒いが、外では猫をかぶっている。 (He is rough at home but plays innocent in public.)

猿も木から落ちる

さるもきからおちる

Even monkeys fall from trees

"Even experts fail"

Anyone, no matter how skilled, can make a mistake.
名医がまさかのミス。猿も木から落ちるね。 (Even a great doctor made a mistake. Anyone can fail.)

鰻登り

うなぎのぼり

Climbing like an eel

"Skyrocketing"

Rising rapidly and steeply.
新商品の売れ行きが鰻登りだ。 (The sales of the new product are skyrocketing.)

炎上する

えんじょうする

Going up in flames

"Social media backlash"

Huge criticism or controversy online.
失言がネットで炎上した。 (The slip of the tongue caused a huge backlash online.)

芽が出る

めがでる

Sprouting a bud

"Showing signs of success"

Efforts finally starting to show results.
下積み時代を経て、ようやく芽が出た。 (After years of struggling, I'm finally seeing success.)

気が短い

きがみじかい

Short energy

"Short-tempered"

Losing patience quickly.
彼は気が短いので、すぐ怒る。 (He is short-tempered, so he gets angry easily.)

花より団子

はなよりだんご

Dumplings over flowers

"Practicality over beauty"

Choosing substance over aesthetics.
景色よりも食事。まさに花より団子だ。 (Food over views. It's truly dumplings over flowers.)

伏線回収

ふくせんかいしゅう

Collecting hidden lines

"Payoff of foreshadowing"

Earlier hints finally being explained.
最終回での伏線回収に感動した。 (I was moved by how the foreshadowing paid off in the finale.)

鵜呑みにする

うのみにする

Swallow like a cormorant

"Accept without question"

Believing everything without thinking.
ニュースを鵜呑みにするのは危険だ。 (It's dangerous to accept news without question.)

ネタバレ

ねたばれ

Material leak

"Spoiler"

Revealing the plot of a story.
映画の結末をネタバレされた。 (The ending of the movie was spoiled for me.)

元を取る

もとをとる

Taking the origin

"Get money's worth"

To recoup the costs spent.
食べ放題で元を取ろうと頑張る。 (I'll try my best to get my money's worth at the buffet.)

ぼったくり

ぼったくり

Plucking/Stripping

"Rip-off"

Unfairly high prices charged to customers.
この店はぼったくりだから気をつけろ。 (Be careful, this shop is a rip-off.)

バズる

ばずる

Creating a buzz

"To go viral"

For content on the internet to spread rapidly.
彼女がSNSに投稿した猫の動画が瞬く間にバズった。 (The cat video she posted on social media went viral in no time.)
